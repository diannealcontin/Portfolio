AquaTrack

AquaTrack is a web-based environmental reporting system that allows users to report pollution incidents with photos and location details, while enabling administrators to monitor, manage, and analyze reports for environmental protection and decision-making.

EcoPlan

EcoPlan is a web-based platform for the Davao del Norte that helps schools, barangays, and local groups organize and monitor climate-related activities. It centralizes event, participant, and resource management to make climate action more efficient and transparent.
